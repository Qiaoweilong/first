Spinblade is a unique play-and-earn game that brings back childhood memories. Each player owns fully customizable & modern-looking spinning-tops and battles them in arenas to win prizes.

anatomy

All Spinblades are NFTs of type ERC-721. Each Spinblade is unique, has no duplicates, and cannot be replaced with another Spinblade with the same attributes. Each Spinblade consists of 3 Parts: Power Ring, Metal Disc, and Tip. Each Part is ERC-1155 type NFT.

POWER RING

Power Ring is a primary combat component of a Spinblade. It affects how a Spinblade engages other Spinblades in terms of using its attacks.

METAL DISC

Metal Disc is a metal part in the middle that affects the movement patterns, balance, and weight of a Spinblade.

TIP

Tip is the bottom part that mostly affects the movement pattern and endurance in terms of the time it keeps spinning without any interruption.

types

Spinblades appear in 4 different types: Attack, Defence, Stamina, and Balance. The main element that determines the type of a Spinblade is each Part that builds up that Spinblade.

ATTACK

Attack, as a stat, affects the attacking/combat power of a Spinblade. If a Spinblade's highest stat is Attack, it becomes an Attack type Spinblade.

DEFENCE

Defence indicates the effectiveness of absorbing opposing Spinblades’ attack power. If a Spinblade's highest stat is Defence, it becomes a Defence type Spinblade.

STAMINA

Stamina defines the endurance of a Spinblade in the arena. If a Spinblade's highest stat is Stamina, it becomes a Stamina type Spinblade.

BALANCE

Balance does not exist as a stat. Balance type Spinblades are of the type where Attack, Defence, and Stamina stats are equal.

tokenomics

There are two types of tokens in the game: Spinblade Coin ($SBC), designated as the governance token, and Bolt Token ($BOLT), designated as an in-game utility token.

$SBC (ERC-20) is fungible governance and staking token that gives ownership of Spinblade to $SBC owners, who will vote on the future development of Spinblade. It will be used to purchase NFTs (Spinblades, Parts, Arenas), forge new Spinblades, and enter competitive battles.
